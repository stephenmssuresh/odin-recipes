Some Delicious Buttermilk Fried Chicken

This easy fried chicken recipe is sure to make you and your family' day! Juicy buttermilk marinated chicken with a subtle spicy flavor. This recipe serves 4 but can easily be scaled to serve more if desired. It takes around 30 minutes of prep time, with the chicken requiring a marination time of at least 8 hours.
