Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Some Delicious Buttermilk Fried Chicken

This easy fried chicken recipe is sure to make you and your family' day! Juicy buttermilk marinated chicken with a subtle spicy flavor. This recipe serves 4 but can easily be scaled to serve more if desired. It takes around 30 minutes of prep time, with the chicken requiring a marination time of at least 8 hours.

Ingredients

  • 1 (3 pound) frying chicken, cut up
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon celery seeds
  • Vegetable oil for deep frying
  • 2 cups Kikkoman Tempura Batter Mix

Steps

  1. Wash chicken and pat dry
  2. In a large bowl, stir together buttermilk, garlic powder, onion powder, poultry seasoning and celery seeds
  3. Place chicken in buttermilk mixture and refrigerate at least 8 hours or overnight
  4. In a large frying pan or wok, heat oil to 325 degrees farenheit
  5. Drain chicken in a colander to remove excess buttermilk
  6. Place tempura batter mix in a large paper bag and add the chicken
  7. Close the bag and gently shake it to coat chicken with tempura batter mix
  8. Remove chicken and fry, turning pieces over after 3 minutes
  9. Continue to fry, turning occasionally, until browned on all sides
